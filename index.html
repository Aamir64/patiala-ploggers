About Us
A Community of Young volunteers Striving to Make Patiala, a Plastic-free Zone. The Patiala Ploggers's Volunteers Plans to Drives Every Weekend to Make Plastic-free Zone
Our Vision
We ploggers are the group of volunteers which have a vision to make this earth a better place to live . We all want a healthy and clean environment for us so it's a initiative taken by us . We conduct plogging( picking up litter + jogging )drives on holidays so more people can join hands to clean environment. Our main moto is to promote clean environment and good health .
Our Main Objective Is To Make Patiala Plastic-Free Environment.
We Want to Aware People About Our Community,So That They Can Help Our Community By Using Less Plastic Things
